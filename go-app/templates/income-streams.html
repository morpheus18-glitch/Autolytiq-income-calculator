Frequently Asked Questions

Start with 2-3 income streams and grow from there. Quality matters more than quantity. Focus on streams that align with your skills and time availability. Millionaires average 7 streams, but they built them over time.
Active income requires your time (job, freelance). Passive income earns while you sleep (dividends, rentals). Most passive income requires upfront work or capital. Start with active income to fund passive investments.
All income is taxable, but rates vary. W-2 wages have taxes withheld. Self-employment income has 15.3% SE tax plus income tax. Investment income may qualify for lower capital gains rates. Rental income has unique deductions.
Start by maximizing your primary income. Then add a side hustle using skills you already have. Invest in index funds for passive growth. As you earn more, explore rental properties or building digital products.
