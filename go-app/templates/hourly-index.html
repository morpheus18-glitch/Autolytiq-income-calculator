How to Convert Hourly Wage to Annual Salary

To calculate your annual salary from an hourly wage, multiply your hourly rate by 2,080 (the number of working hours in a year: 40 hours/week × 52 weeks). For example, $25 per hour equals $52,000 per year before taxes.

Quick Formula

Annual Salary = Hourly Rate × 2,080

Why This Matters

Knowing your annual salary helps you budget, qualify for loans, compare job offers, and understand your true earning power. Our breakdowns include federal and state tax estimates so you can see your actual take-home pay.
